# Ibrahim Mahama — Seed Content

> Ghanaian businessman, founder of Engineers & Planners. NOT the artist.

## Identity
- **Full name:** Ibrahim Mahama
- **DOB:** 29 January 1971
- **Birthplace:** Piase, Northern Region, Ghana (raised Tamale; ancestral Damongo / West Gonja)
- **Heritage:** Gonja (paternal) + Ewe (maternal)
- **Religion:** Muslim
- **Wife:** Oona Mahama
- **Children:** 3

## Family
- **Father:** Emmanuel Adama Mahama — first MP for West Gonja, first Regional Commissioner of Northern Region under Nkrumah
- **Mother:** Joyce Tamakloe (Keta, Volta) — d. of breast cancer
- **Brother:** John Dramani Mahama — President of Ghana
- Inter-faith family — Christians and Muslims; Ibrahim donates to both communities

## Education
- Tamale Senior High School (TAMASCO)
- College of North West London, UK — business and construction
- Worked in London property development before returning to Ghana 1997
- (NOT USSR — that's a misconception)

## Career
- **1997** — Founds Engineers & Planners (E&P) on return from UK
- **2004** — Asutsuare Poultry Farms
- **2011** — Begins Dzata Cement plant construction in Tema
- **2018** — Dzata Cement begins commercial operations; named African Industrialist of the Year (African Achievers Awards, House of Commons, London)
- **2020** — Acquires all ordinary shares in Cardinal Resources (Australian-listed gold)
- **2022** — Ultimate Man of the Year (EMY Africa Awards)
- **April 2026** — E&P wins Damang Mining Lease tender; takes over operations from Gold Fields after 30 years

## Companies
- **Engineers & Planners (E&P)** — flagship; ~3,000–4,000+ employees; subsidiaries in Liberia, Sierra Leone; mining services, civil works, equipment rental. Motto: "AD ASTRA PER ASPERA — To the sky through thick and thin." / "We go where others fear to tread." / "E&P Can Do."
- **Dzata Cement Limited** — Ghana's first wholly Ghanaian-owned cement manufacturer; 2M tonnes/yr; ~1,200 jobs
- **Asutsuare Poultry Farms** — ~150,000 eggs/day, ~10,000 broilers/day
- **Man Bosch Ghana Limited (MBG)** — heavy equipment; MAN Diesel Trucks, Renault Trucks rep
- **Cardinal Resources / Cardinal Gold Mine** (acquired 2020)
- **Damang Gold Mine** — operator from April 2026
- **Aviation** — owns Bombardier Challenger 604 named "Dzata" (Gonja: lion); reportedly first Ghanaian to do so

## Philanthropy

### Health
- **Joyce Tamakloe Cancer Foundation** — co-founded for late mother; >1,000 free mammograms; oncology unit support
- COVID 2020: Drager Fabius Plus anaesthetic machine to UGMC + PPE
- 2024: $25,000 for Rosemary Boadu's kidney transplant (age 13)

### COVID-19 (2020)
- Donations to National Chief Imam, St James Catholic Osu, Christ the King, ~10,000 households
- 5,000 bags of rice, 12,000 bottles cooking oil, 20,000 cans mackerel
- Handwashing machines to UG-Legon, Islamic University Ghana, UG Medical School, others
- PPE pledge to Tamale Teaching Hospital

### Education
- TAMASCO: 550-bed dormitory after fire; 1,000 bags rice + 100 bottles oil + 200 boxes mackerel + 200 boxes tomato paste (2022); 35-seater bus (Nov 2024)
- Presbyterian SHS: 500 bags cement (June 2023)
- Northern Region & Zongo scholarships and infrastructure

### Disasters
- 2023 Akosombo Dam spillage relief — Volta and Eastern regions

### Sports
- ~35% stake in Tamale City FC (Ghana Premier League)
- Public patron of Black Stars and Ghanaian football
